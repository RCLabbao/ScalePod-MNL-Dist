The Numbers Don’t Lie

  • A 1-second delay in page load reduces conversions by 7%
  • 53% of mobile users abandon sites that take over 3 seconds to load
  • Google uses Core Web Vitals as a ranking factor

Every millisecond of load time is literally costing you money.

How We Optimize Shopify Store Speed

Image Optimization

We implement lazy loading, responsive images, and next-gen formats (WebP, AVIF). Product images are the heaviest assets on most Shopify stores.

Liquid Optimization

We audit and optimize your theme’s Liquid code — removing unnecessary loops, caching fragments, and reducing database queries.

Third-Party Script Management

Analytics, chat widgets, and review apps are often the biggest performance culprits. We audit, defer, and lazy-load every third-party script.

Critical Rendering Path

We inline critical CSS, defer non-essential stylesheets, and prioritize above-the-fold content to achieve fast First Contentful Paint times.

Real Results

Our clients see an average load time reduction of 60%. For a store doing $1M/month in revenue, that can translate to an additional $70K-$100K/month in sales.
